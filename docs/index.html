Welcome to plotly-resampler’s documentation!#

This is the documentation of plotly-resampler; a wrapper for plotly Figures to visualize large time-series data.



As shown in the demo above, plotly-resampler maintains its interactiveness on large data by applying front-end resampling respective to the view.
